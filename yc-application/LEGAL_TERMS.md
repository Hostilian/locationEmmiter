# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

## 1. Privacy Policy (GDPR/CCPA/LGPD Compliant)
**Last Updated: 2026-05-07**

- **Data Sovereignty**: Location Emitter is designed as a "Local-First" application. Precise location data is stored locally on the user's device and only transmitted over peer-to-peer LoRa/BLE mesh networks.
- **Data Collection**: We do not collect, store, or sell personally identifiable information (PII) on central servers unless the user explicitly opts into the "Cloud Sync" service.
- **BLE & GPS Permissions**: The application requires Bluetooth and Location permissions to interface with LoRa hardware and provide real-time mapping. This data is processed locally and not uploaded to any third-party analytics without consent.
- **User Rights**: 
  - **Right to Access**: Users can view all local data via the app.
  - **Right to Erasure**: Users can wipe all local and mesh-relay data at any time via the "Clear History" button.
  - **No Sale of Data**: We do not sell user location history to third parties (CCPA "Do Not Sell" compliance).

## 2. End User License Agreement (EULA)
- **Emergency Use**: While Location Emitter includes SOS features, it is NOT a replacement for professional emergency services (911, SAR). It relies on best-effort LoRa mesh delivery. Use at your own risk in life-critical situations.
- **Encryption**: Users are responsible for managing their private group keys. Lost keys cannot be recovered by Location Emitter contributors.
- **Compliance**: Users must comply with local RF regulations (FCC/ETSI) regarding duty cycle and transmission power. The software provides tools for compliance but the final responsibility lies with the operator.
