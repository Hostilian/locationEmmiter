# App Store Metadata & Marketing Assets

This document contains the finalized metadata and asset descriptions for the **Location Emitter (Peer Map)** v1.1.0 release.

## 1. App Store Details

- **App Name**: Location Emitter: Peer Map
- **Short Description**: Off-grid LoRa mesh tracking and SOS.
- **Full Description**: 
  Location Emitter turns your phone into a powerful off-grid navigation and safety tool. By pairing with compatible ESP32-LoRa hardware, you can track teammates, send secure mesh messages, and broadcast SOS alerts without cellular service or internet.
  
  Key Features:
  - **Local-First Privacy**: Your location stays on your device and your private mesh.
  - **LoRa Mesh Integration**: Connect to Heltec, T-Beam, and other LoRa boards via BLE.
  - **Real-time Mapping**: View peers on high-performance vector maps with offline support.
  - **SOS Broadcasting**: One-tap emergency alerts visible to everyone in the mesh.
  - **Multilingual**: Support for English, French, and Spanish.

- **Keywords**: LoRa, Mesh Network, Off-grid, Tracking, SOS, Navigation, GPS, ESP32, T-Beam, Privacy.
